Banana Bread

Description

This recipe is a favorite in our household. Moist, delicious and absolutely packed with banana flavor.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in the oven preheated to 350 for 1 hour.
